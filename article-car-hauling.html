How Much Can You Make Car Hauling?

Car haul earnings depend on equipment (open vs enclosed), lane, season, and how tight your damage/claims process is. Top operators focus on load quality and minimizing rework.

What moves the number

  • Deck configuration and height clearances
  • Broker relationships and OEM or dealer lanes
  • Insurance and cargo claim history
