FMCSA Speed Limiter Rulemaking: How to Prepare Your Fleet

When rulemaking touches vehicle speed, fleets should review telematics settings, safety policies, and driver communication — even before a final rule is published.

Practical preparation

  • Audit what your ELD/telematics provider can report about governed speed
  • Align safety messaging with actual company expectations on highways
  • Document maintenance for speed-related equipment if required
