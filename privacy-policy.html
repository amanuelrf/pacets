Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

At Pace Transportation Services, LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you interact with us, including when you provide your phone number for texting, emails, or website inquiries.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, phone number, email address, and company information
  • Payment or invoice information related to freight factoring transactions
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer service
  • Send messages, including SMS/text messages, regarding invoices, payments, or updates
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers, such as RingCentral, to deliver texting and communications services
  • Business partners necessary to complete factoring transactions
  • Law enforcement or regulatory agencies when required by law

4. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information.

5. Your Consent & Opt-Out

By providing your phone number or contacting us, you consent to receiving communications, including SMS/text messages, from Pace Transportation Services, LLC. You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying “STOP” to a text or contacting us at the information below.

6. Privacy Policy Disclaimer

SMS opt-in or phone numbers for the purpose of SMS are not being shared with any third party and affiliate company for marketing purposes.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on our website.
